Broad Cast Method

This is the most common method of fertilizer application,
where the fertilizer is spread uniformly over the entire field surface. Broadcasting can be done by hand or with a mechanical spreader.

Learn more

Top Dressing and Side dressing

Spreading of fertilizer in standing crops without considering the crop rows is termed as a
top dressing. However, when the crop rows are taken into account, and the material is dropped on the ground surface near the crop rows, it is called
side dressing.

Learn more

Plough sole placement

When the fertilizers are applied in open furrows at plow sole level while plowing,
it is termed plow sole placement. Such furrows are covered immediately during the next run of the plow.

Irrigation and Water Conservation Techniques

Surface Irrigation

Surface irrigation is a type of irrigation method that involves
applying water to the soil surface and allowing it to infiltrate and move through the soil by gravity. Surface irrigation can be divided into several subtypes, including:

  • Flood Irrigation
  • Furrow Irrigation
  • Basin Irrigation
  • Border Irrigation

Learn more

Drip Irrigation

SDrip irrigation is a type of irrigation method that involves
applying water directly to the roots of plants through a network of small tubes or emitters.
Drip irrigation systems can be divided into several subtypes, including:

  • Point source drip
  • Subsurface drip
  • Micro-sprinkler
