FAQ'S

What is SmartMavuno?

SmartMavuno is a platform that connects small-scale farmers in Africa with information, resources, and markets to help them improve their productivity, profitability, and sustainability.

How does SmartMavuno work?

SmartMavuno uses a combination of mobile technology, data analytics, and partnerships with local organizations to provide farmers with access to information on weather, soil health, crop management, and more. It also connects farmers with markets to help them sell their products and earn a fair price.

What types of information and resources does SmartMavuno provide?

SmartMavuno provides a range of information and resources to help farmers improve their productivity and sustainability. This includes weather forecasts, soil health analysis, crop management advice, and access to markets and finance.
